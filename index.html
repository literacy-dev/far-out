How to Play

Directions

👉 Collect stardust for points and meteors for ammo as your rocket explores the galaxy.

👉 Dodge or fire meteors at enemy UFOs while nearby forces affect your speed.

👉 Grab a donut to live longer*

*I am not a doctor.

Items:

  • 1 point
  • ammo
  • extra life

CONTROLS

Keyboard

  • UP: 'K'
  • DOWN: 'D'
  • FIRE: 'Spacebar'

Touchscreen

  • UP & DOWN: Tap top & bottom of screen behind rocket
  • FIRE: Tap anywhere in front of rocket
